San Antonio, TX Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Explore the Riverwalk

Discover the beauty of San Antonio's famous Riverwalk, lined with restaurants, shops, and scenic views.

Day 2: The Alamo

Visit the historic Alamo, a symbol of Texas independence, and learn about its rich history.

Day 3: Art and Culture

Explore the art and culture of San Antonio with visits to museums and galleries.

Day 4: Outdoor Adventure

Get outdoors and enjoy activities like hiking, biking, or a visit to a nearby park.

Day 5: Culinary Delights

Indulge in San Antonio's diverse culinary scene with delicious local dishes.

Day 6: Shopping Excursion

Shop for souvenirs and explore local markets and shopping districts.

Day 7: History Tour

Take a tour of historic sites and landmarks that shaped the city's past.

Day 8: Relax and Depart

Relax, enjoy your last day, and prepare for your departure from San Antonio.
